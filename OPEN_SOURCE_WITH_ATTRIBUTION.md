### Q: Is AEVA really free for commercial use?
**A**: Yes! You can use AEVA in commercial products. Just notify us and provide attribution.

### Q: Do I need to open source my project if I use AEVA?
**A**: No. AEVA is permissive. Your project can be closed source or open source.

### Q: What if I modify AEVA's code?
**A**: You can modify it freely. Keep the original watermark and add your own copyright for your changes.

**A**: No. Copyright headers and watermarks must remain intact.

**A**: We appreciate notification to build community, but it's primarily a courtesy. Attribution and watermark are the key requirements.
